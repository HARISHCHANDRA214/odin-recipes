Biryani Recipe

This Chicken Biryani recipe is prepared using basmati rice, chicken thighs, hung curd, onion, tomato, milk, saffron and a melange of whole and ground spices. For preparing this amazing and mouth-watering Biryani recipe, chicken is marinated in yoghurt and a mix of spices and then cooked using the slow-cooking method.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup boiled basmati rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon mint leaves
  • 1 teaspoon saffron
  • salt as required
  • 2 teaspoon virgin olive oil
  • 3 green cardamom
  • 2 clove
  • 2 onion
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • chicken
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala powder
  • 4 green chillies
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric

How to make Chicken Biryani step-by-step

  1. Prepare saffron-kewra water and chop veggies.
  2. Saute the onions.
  3. Cook biryani on low heat for 5-6 minutes.
  4. Serve hot chicken biryani with your favorite chutney or raita.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
